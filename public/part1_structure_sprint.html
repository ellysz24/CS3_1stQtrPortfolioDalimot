Recipe Page DALIMOT, Kathrin Ellysz of 9 - Potassium

My Favorite Recipe

This is my favorite recipe because it's the first meal I cooked myself.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • 500G Ground Beef
  • 2 Bulbs Crushed Garlic
  • 1 Canned Mushroom[Stems and Pieces]
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 2 Canned Peeled Tomatoes
  • 1/2 Tsp. Oregano Powder
  • 3 Chicken Cubes
  • 1 Tbsp. Red Pepper Flakes
  • Fresh Basil
  • Grated Parmesan
  • 500G Penne Pasta

Instructions:

  1. Saute garlic in olive oil under low heat.
  2. Add the ground beef, cook in high heat and wait for oil to come out.
  3. Add some salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Add mushroom, 1 chicken cube and tomato, mix well.
  5. Add red pepper flakes, oregano powder, dried basil and 2 more chicken cubes.
  6. Turn down to medium heat until more oil from the ground beef comes out.
  7. While waiting for the ground beef to cook, prepare the pasta. Check the cooking instructions of the pasta used.
  8. Boil water, add salt and oil, then the pasta.
  9. Cook the pasta for 11 minutes
  10. When the ground beef mixture is cooked already, add the fresh basil and grated parmesan cheese then turn off the heat.
  11. Lastly, add the pasta in the sauce and mix well.
  12. Garnish, serve, and enjoy your Meaty Penne Arrabbiata!
